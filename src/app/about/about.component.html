Founded by Mariana Menano and Gonçalo Diniz in 2022.

The practice has gained an international reputation for its

housing, cultural, educational and mixed-use projects.

Sometimes described as architect detectives, the studio finds

new connections between materials, landscape, climate, history

and culture. Drawing on social histories, construction traditions,

contemporary art and fashion, projects emerge firmly rooted in

their place.

Menano Diniz is the product of longstanding collaborations with

engineers, horticulturalists and artists. Teaching and research is

a continuous backdrop to the studio. Tom Emerson is professor

of architecture and Dean of the Department of Architecture, eth

Zurich where he leads a research and design studio exploring the

relationship between making and the territory.
